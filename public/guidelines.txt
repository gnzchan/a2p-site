A2P 10DLC COMPLIANCE GUIDELINE
================================================================================

REQUIRED PAGES
• Homepage - business info, address, phone
• SMS Opt-in Page - dedicated consent form
• Contact Page - with opt-in option
• Privacy Policy - A2P section required
• Terms of Service - SMS terms section required

OPT-IN FORM REQUIREMENTS
• Fields: name, phone (validated), email
• Consent checkbox: separate, unchecked by default, required to submit
• Validation: prevent submission without consent
• Error message: "Please check the consent box to continue"

CONSENT LANGUAGE (on form)
"I consent to receive SMS messages from [COMPANY] related to [PURPOSE].
Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to
unsubscribe, HELP for help. See our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy."

• Must link to Terms and Privacy Policy
• Cannot bundle SMS consent with other agreements

MANDATORY DISCLOSURES (all opt-in forms)
• Message frequency: "varies" or "based on use case" or specific number
• Cost: "Message & data rates may apply"
• STOP: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe" + phone number
• HELP: "Reply HELP for help" + phone number
• Links to Terms and Privacy Policy

BUSINESS INFORMATION (visible on site)
• Legal business name + type (LLC, Corp, etc.)
• Complete physical address (street, city, state, zip)
• Primary phone number (for HELP keyword)
• Business email address
• Display on: homepage, contact page, footer, legal docs

PRIVACY POLICY - REQUIRED SECTIONS
1. Information We Collect
   - SMS subscription data, contact info, preferences

2. SMS Text Message Service
   - Specific use case description
   - "Mobile information will NOT be shared with third parties for marketing"
   - Message frequency, cost disclosure
   - "Reply STOP to opt out" + "Text HELP to [PHONE]"

3. Information Sharing
   - "We do not sell, trade, or transfer personal information to third parties
     for marketing purposes"

4. A2P 10DLC Compliance (dedicated section)
   - Compliance with A2P 10DLC and TCPA
   - Practices: explicit opt-in, clear opt-out, transparent disclosures,
     proper registration

5. Contact Information
   - Full business details (name, address, phone, email)

6. Last Updated date

TERMS OF SERVICE - REQUIRED SECTIONS
1. SMS Text Message Service Terms
   - "By providing your mobile number and checking the consent box, you
     expressly consent to receive SMS from [COMPANY] for [USE CASE]"
   - Message frequency, "Message & data rates may apply"
   - "Carriers not liable for delayed/undelivered messages"
   - "Text STOP to [PHONE] to opt out"
   - "Text HELP to [PHONE] for support"
   - "You will receive confirmation when you opt out"

2. A2P 10DLC Compliance
   - "Our SMS services comply with A2P 10DLC and TCPA regulations"
   - Practices: explicit consent required, clear opt-out, consent records
     maintained, messages only for stated purposes

3. Privacy and Data Protection
   - "We will never sell or share your mobile number with third parties for
     marketing purposes"
   - Reference Privacy Policy

4. Contact Information + Governing Law
5. Last Updated date

TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION
• Centralized config file for all business info (single source of truth)
• Consent validation: check before submission, log timestamp
• All pages use HTTPS
• Consistent business info across all pages
• Header with nav to legal pages
• Footer with business info + legal links on every page

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID
❌ Pre-checked consent boxes
❌ Bundling SMS consent with other terms
❌ Missing STOP/HELP keywords or phone number
❌ No frequency/cost disclosure
❌ Incomplete business address
❌ Missing legal doc links (privacy and terms)
❌ Inconsistent business info across pages
❌ Form submits without consent
❌ Using opt-out instead of opt-in
❌ Vague consent language
❌ Outdated legal docs

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
• A2P 10DLC (Application-to-Person 10-Digit Long Code)
• TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act)
• Register business with TCR (The Campaign Registry)
• Complete 10DLC campaign registration
• Get carrier approval

KEY PRINCIPLES
• Explicit opt-in only (never opt-out)
• Keep consent separate from other terms
• Use plain language
• Never pre-check consent boxes
• Display disclosures prominently
• Never share phone numbers with third parties for marketing
• Honor STOP requests immediately
• Store consent records with timestamps
• Keep legal docs current
• Compliance is ongoing, not one-time
